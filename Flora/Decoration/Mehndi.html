MEHNDI DECORATION

Mehndi is considered to be the most important function at wedding ceremony in Asian regions. Everyone tries to make the mehndi function the most unforgettable one. Houses are decorated with different items on mehndi. Mehndi decorations are done with different items, stages are decorated and lots of fun is there on the mehndi function. The items we used for mehndi decoration includes:Flowers, bouquets, floral altars, beautiful couches, mehndi trays and candles.
